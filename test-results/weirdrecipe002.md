Cooking plan
•
Tonight we are making Italian Ground beef, Garlic & Mushrooms Composed Plate. It is planned for about 26 minutes and high energy. We will take it one stage at a time.

•
Before you begin, gather the ingredients and equipment for Ground beef & Garlic & Mushrooms.

1
Minutes 0–4: Ingredient Prep:

- Brush or quickly rinse attached dirt from Mushrooms, dry well, trim the stem ends and cut evenly.

- Keep raw Ground beef separate from ready-to-eat food.

Season with Black pepper.

- Prepare Garlic in the form recorded in My Kitchen; mince fresh forms evenly.

- Measure 1 1/2 cups Tomato sauce.

Measure 1 tablespoon Butter.

Measure 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper.

2
Minutes 4–12: Cook Mushrooms in an uncrowded layer until released moisture evaporates and the bottoms brown; turn and finish cooking.

Aim for deeply browned mushrooms without pooled water.

Stop when moisture has evaporated and broad surfaces are browned and tender.

Transfer Mushrooms to a plate and keep it warm before reusing the skillet.

You will need about 6 minutes of attention during this window.

3
Minutes 12–13: Add Garlic to hot fat and stir just until fragrant, about 30–60 seconds, before adding wetter ingredients.

Aim for fragrant without dark or bitter edges.

Stop when strong aroma appears before visible burning.

Transfer Garlic to a plate and keep it warm before reusing the skillet.

4
Minutes 13–21: Add Ground beef to the hot skillet and break it into small crumbles.

Cook until no raw areas remain, then verify safe doneness.

Aim for evenly browned, moist crumbles.

Stop when the center of the thickest clump reaches the ingredient's safe temperature.

You will need about 7 minutes of attention during this window.

5
Minutes 21–24: Add the tomato sauce and seasonings to the browned cooking flavor.

Simmer gently until hot and slightly reduced.

Taste before adding salt.

The sauce is ready.

6
Minutes 24–26: Plate Garlic & Mushrooms with the finished sauce.

Add Ground beef to the plates and serve immediately.

•
Nicely done. Dinner is ready.
