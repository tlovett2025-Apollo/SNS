Timeline Intelligence shifted the planner's focus from sequencing ingredients to orchestrating activities.
Meal duration and ingredient duration are distinct engineering concepts.
Knowledge Objects should ultimately publish activities; the planner should not infer them.
Activities became the emerging common language between the CKB and the planner.
Customer Success / Knowledge Manager was identified as a future organizational role responsible for customer education, trend analysis, and shielding Engineering from routine support.
Product philosophy reaffirmed: Every architectural decision should reduce stress in someone's kitchen.
Mission refinement: SNS is not designed for competitive cooking; it is designed for overwhelmed households who need confidence, guidance, and realistic meal coordination.
