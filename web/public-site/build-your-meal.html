Before you cook

This cooking plan assumes the selected protein is fully thawed before Step 1.

Refrigerator: Plan ahead. A small package commonly needs about a day.

Cold water: Keep it in a leak-proof bag in cold water, changing the water every 30 minutes. A small one-pound package may thaw in an hour or less. Cook it immediately.

Microwave: Use the defrost setting and follow your appliance instructions. Cook it immediately.
